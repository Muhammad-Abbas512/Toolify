How to Convert Images Online

Our Image Converter allows you to transform images between different formats while maintaining quality. Perfect for web optimization, compatibility, and storage reduction.

Supported Image Formats

  • JPG/JPEG: Best for photographs and complex images with many colors
  • PNG: Ideal for images with transparency or simple graphics
  • WebP: Modern format with excellent compression for web use
  • GIF: For simple animations and low-color images
  • BMP: Uncompressed format for maximum quality
  • ICO: For website favicons and application icons

Why Convert Images?

Image conversion serves several important purposes:

  • Web Optimization: Convert to WebP or optimize JPG for faster loading
  • Compatibility: Convert to widely supported formats for sharing
  • Storage Reduction: Compress images to save disk space
  • Professional Requirements: Meet specific format requirements for print or web
  • Batch Processing: Convert multiple images at once to save time

Quality Settings Guide

  1. Maximum Quality (90-100%): For printing or archival purposes
  2. High Quality (75-90%): Ideal for websites and digital displays
  3. Medium Quality (50-75%): Good balance for web use
  4. Low Quality (25-50%): For thumbnails or quick previews
  5. Minimum Quality (1-25%): For extremely small file sizes

Resizing Tips

  • Always maintain aspect ratio to prevent distortion
  • For web use, resize to the exact dimensions needed
  • For printing, maintain at least 300 DPI resolution
  • Use square dimensions for profile pictures and avatars
  • Downsize large images rather than upsizing small ones

Is Image Conversion Safe?

Yes! All conversion happens locally in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. Your images never leave your computer. We don't store, share, or have access to your files.

Common Questions

Q: Will converting images reduce quality?
A: With proper settings, quality loss is minimal. JPG compression causes some loss, while PNG is lossless.

Q: Can I convert multiple images at once?
A: Yes! Our tool supports batch conversion of up to 50 images simultaneously.

Q: What's the maximum image size?
A: We support images up to 25MB each. For larger files, consider resizing first.

Q: Does WebP support animation?
A: Yes, WebP supports both static and animated images like GIF.

Q: Can I convert SVG files?
A: Yes, SVG files are supported and can be converted to raster formats.
